## The Challenge

The Neuroscience department needed precise digital tools to administer cognitive tests, replacing manual methods that introduced variability into measurements.

## The Solution

With full autonomy, I developed specialized software tools that digitize standard neuropsychological tests. The system captures reaction times with sub-millisecond precision and generates datasets compatible with the department's statistical analysis tools.

## Technical Details

- **Temporal precision:** Sub-millisecond reaction time measurement
- **Protocols:** Faithful implementation of standardized neuropsychological tests
- **Export:** Data compatible with SPSS and MATLAB
- **UX:** Simple interface for non-technical participants
