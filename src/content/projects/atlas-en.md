## The Challenge

Create a VR experience that goes beyond surface-level entertainment, offering technical puzzles that require critical thinking and realistic physical manipulation of objects.

## The Solution

Using Unity with Hurricane VR for physical interactions, I built a detailed spaceship where every system (life support, navigation, communications) can fail and requires specific repair procedures. The player must diagnose issues, locate tools, and execute repairs under time pressure.

## Technical Details

- **Engine:** Unity with a rendering pipeline optimized for VR
- **Interactions:** Hurricane VR for physical object manipulation
- **Puzzles:** Modular crisis system with multiple possible solutions
- **Audio:** 3D spatial sound design for full immersion
