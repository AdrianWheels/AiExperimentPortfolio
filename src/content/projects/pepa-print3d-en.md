## The Challenge

The business sold primarily through social media and needed a professional website that showcased their catalog attractively and allowed customers to place orders directly, without the overhead of a complex e-commerce setup.

## The Solution

I chose Astro to generate a fully static site with instant loading. The design uses gradients and colors inspired by multicolor 3D printing filaments. The catalog is filterable by category, and each product has a direct order form.

## Technical Details

- **Framework:** Astro with static generation (zero client-side JavaScript)
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS with a custom color system
- **Forms:** FormSubmit for orders without a backend
- **Typing:** TypeScript for full type safety
- **Deployment:** Static hosting with global CDN
