## The Challenge

The Psychology department needed innovative assessment tools that could overcome the limitations of traditional paper-based tests, especially with hard-to-evaluate populations such as juvenile offenders.

## The Solution

With full autonomy, I designed and built virtual environments that present social and decision-making scenarios. The system records metrics such as response time, gaze direction, and movement patterns, providing richer data than traditional questionnaires.

## Technical Details

- **Engine:** Unity with full VR tracking
- **Measurement:** Telemetry system for behavioral data collection
- **Protocol:** Designed following ethical standards for research involving minors
- **Integration:** Compatible with the department's analysis pipeline
